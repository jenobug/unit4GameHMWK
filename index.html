FLOWER COLLECTOR

LUCKY NUMBER:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Click on a flower to add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each flower is hidden until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each flower.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
